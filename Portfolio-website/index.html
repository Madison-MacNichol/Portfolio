About me

Madison is a recent graduate in the communication field, and a soon to be Jr. Software Developer. She is currently a student at The Tech Academy in Portland, Oregon, but being from rural Idaho and having lived in various parts of the world, she is aware how valuable communication can be. In today's world, one way this communication happens is via technology. The collision of these two fields has led her into the world of software development and web design as a way of assisting individuals and organizations work effectively together, across town or around the world.

In her consistently shrinking free time, she enjoys a multitude of hobbies including: running, swimming, biking and working on obedience skills with her dog, Charlie. She also likes to continue her education especially in the foreign language feild as human and computer languages are her continuing passion!
