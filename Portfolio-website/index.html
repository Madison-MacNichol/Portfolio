About me

Madison is a recent graduate in the communication field, and a soon to be Jr. Software Developer. Madison is currently a student at The Tech Academy in Portland, Oregon. Being from rural Idaho and having lived in various parts of the world, she is aware how valuable intercultural communication can be. Theses days, one way this communication happens is via technology. The collision of these two fields has led her into the world of software development and website design as a way of assisting individuals and organizations interface effectively with each other, across town or around the world.

In her consistently shrinking free time, she enjoys a multitude of hobbies including but not limited to: running, swimming, biking and working on obedience skills with her dog, Charlie. She also likes to continue her education especially in the foreign language field, along with reading and blogging when the weather is too rainy to be outside.
